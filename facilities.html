The Hotel

Located in the beating heart of the Cardiff Bay area. Our hotel boasts 158 top of the range, custom built capsules, designed with your comfort in mind. Enjoy our on-site parking, free Wi-Fi, 24/7 concierge service and a host of other facilities to ensure your visit comes equipped with peace of mind.

Right at the epicentre of Mermaid Quay, Cardiff Bay's waterfront shopping and leisure district. There's a host of restaurants, bars and entertainment at your fingertips. Within walking distance of the Red Dragon Centre and the Wales Millennium Centre, you'll be primed to explore a plethora of local hotspots.

Transportation links into Cardiff City Centre are conveniently located nearby at the Cardiff Bay train station. You'll be taking in the sights before you know it with quick connections just over a mile away. Regular buses also stop close by at Cardiff County Hall, a short distance from the hotel. The very best of what Cardiff has to offer is within your grasp.

Single Capsule

Our single capsules come fully equipped with state-of-the-art security, entertainment, and convenient features. Including free Wi-Fi, a personal deposit box, TV, and much more. With a luxurious memory foam mattress and premium quality bedding you'll feel like you're sleeping on a cloud. And with every pod featuring a top of the range soundproofing membrane, you'll be ensured a silent night of total relaxation.

Prices starting from £30 per night.
* Excluding breakfast

Double Capsule

Looking to share your experience with someone else or maybe you just feel like splashing out for extra space? Then the double capsule is for you! Hosting all the same amenities as its smaller sibling but with the added interior space to house a king size bed. This is the pinnacle of capsule pod comfort.

Prices starting from £45 per night.
